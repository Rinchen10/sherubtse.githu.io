Academic Workshops

Sherubtse College offers academic workshops focused on enhancing students' knowledge and skills. Topics include research methodologies, writing techniques, and interdisciplinary studies. These workshops foster a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and academic excellence.

Recent workshops include:

  • Research Methodology for Undergraduate Students
  • Technology and Data Analysis in Modern Education
  • Effective Communication for Academic Writing

Cultural Festivals

Celebrating Bhutanese heritage is a core value at Sherubtse College. Cultural festivals feature traditional dances, songs, food stalls, and art exhibits, promoting a sense of community and pride in Bhutan's rich traditions.

Notable events include:

  • Annual Bhutanese Food Festival
  • Traditional Mask Dance Performances
  • Art and Craft Exhibitions by Students

Leadership Seminars

Sherubtse College hosts leadership seminars to prepare students for challenges in the real world. Sessions include guest lectures by professionals, workshops on ethical leadership, and discussions on sustainability.

Key highlights include:

  • Leadership in Environmental Conservation
  • Strategies for Community Development
  • Interactive Workshops on Ethical Decision-Making

Sports Competitions

Sports play a vital role in holistic development at Sherubtse. The college organizes various sports tournaments to encourage teamwork, physical fitness, and friendly competition among students.

Popular events include:

  • Annual Inter-Department Football Tournament
  • Basketball Championship
  • Track and Field Competitions

Career Guidance

Sherubtse College provides comprehensive career guidance services, including counseling sessions, job fairs, and skill-building workshops. These initiatives aim to prepare students for their professional journeys.

Key services include:

  • Resume Writing and Interview Preparation Workshops
  • Internship and Placement Opportunities
  • Alumni Network and Mentorship Programs
